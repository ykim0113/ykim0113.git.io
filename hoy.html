PAIN POINT

"How do we keep track of long-term goals?"

It started out as my classmate sharing his problem of having a hard time keeping up with various tasks during the semester. He brought up how he faced difficutly especially keeping track of long-term goals, and wished to find a easier way to manage his tasks.

SOLUTION

Thus we attempted to resolve the problem by designing and creating a customized to-do app.

Designed for both iOS and Android, Hoy is a to-do list app that manages the tasks for the users so they can easily track their long-term goals, stay on schedule for repetitive tasks, and prioritize their tasks for the day to increase the user's productivity.

EVALUATION

Due to limitations on time and location, I could not fully conduct full usability tests on Hoy. However I let two of my interview participants try out the prototype and they reacted positively. In addition their feedbacks helped improving the UI for the main features of the app.
