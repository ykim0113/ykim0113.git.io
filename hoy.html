Hoy

Focus on today to accomplish long-term goals.

Overview

During my sophomore year summer break, I came across my friend’s problem which I emphasized a lot with as a fellow college student. He was worried about becoming a Junior and having to balance hard classes with work and social life. He has been using productivity apps to stay organized but he said they didn’t help him too much to accomplish his long term goals.

My friend had an idea of for making a new version of a productivity app and I wanted to help him contextualize the problem and come up with a viable solution. The initial outcome was to build a productivity app for long-term goals and for me was to design user-friendly features and design system of the app’s UI.

Duration

Role: UI/UX Design

Jun - Jul 2018

Tools: Sketch, InVision

PAIN POINT

"How do we keep track of long-term goals?"

It started out as my classmate sharing his problem of having a hard time keeping up with various tasks during the semester. He brought up how he faced difficutly especially keeping track of long-term goals, and wished to find a easier way to manage his tasks.

SOLUTION

Thus we attempted to resolve the problem by designing and creating a customized to-do app.

Designed for both iOS and Android, Hoy is a to-do list app that manages the tasks for the users so they can easily track their long-term goals, stay on schedule for repetitive tasks, and prioritize their tasks for the day to increase the user's productivity.

RESEARCH

> User Interview - Survey questions

To understand the user's pain points and gather his or her requests, I decided to interview our perspective user, my classmate.

The crucial point in the interview was to understand what a long-term task meant to him and his problems regarding it. Thus the questions aimed to cover the following points:

  • Difference between a long-term and a short-term goal (Duration, Timeline, Frequency)
  • Reasons to use a to-do list (Work, Personal Life, Both)
  • How a to-do list would help him (Productivity, Organization, Motivation)
  • Familiarity with using to-do list (Digital or Analog)

Then based on the interview results, I categorized the interviewees answers. This process helped me proritize his wants and needs, which would be reflected as main features for the app.

EVALUATION

Due to limitations on time and location, I could not fully conduct full usability tests on Hoy. However I let two of my interview participants try out the prototype and they reacted positively. In addition their feedbacks helped improving the UI for the main features of the app.
